## Tips for Success

**Be honest about patterns:**
The Oracle only works if you're honest about your behavioral patterns. Don't hide the embarrassing stuff - that's where the value is.

**Update regularly:**
When new facts emerge (payment received, client lost, pattern recognized), update the relevant MD files immediately.

**Trust the consensus:**
When all 5 advisors agree, that's your signal. Don't overthink it.

**Use accountability questions:**
Pick ONE question that matters most. Ask it weekly. Track the answer.

**Respect your patterns:**
The Oracle works WITH who you are, not against it. If you have marketing resistance, it finds ways to market that feel tolerable.
